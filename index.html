Primes around X

Prime test, factorization, largest prime < X, smallest prime > X. SymPy handles large integers; very large factorizations can take a long time or exhaust memory. 


        
      


      

        
Max divisors ≤ X

        

          Maximum N ≤ X such that
          d(N) is largest among
          {1,…,X} (ties → larger N). Uses an
          O(X log X) divisor sieve;
          X ≤ 2·10⁶ in this build.
